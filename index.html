TILA Reconciliation

Our solution addresses discrepancies between the production system and TILA (Truth in Lending Act) disclosures.

The process involves two stages:

Stage 1: Underwriting Review Compares key loan related charges communicated to customers in TILA with actual charges as per production system data. Highlights differences to ensure accurate and transparent charges levied on customer.
Stage 2: Customer Management Analysis

Compares loan charges levied on customers with the information mentioned in TILA Identifies discrepancies to ensure customers are not charged more than what they were informed as per TILA
